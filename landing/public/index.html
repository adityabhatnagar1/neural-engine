What actually is NEURAL ENGINE?!

NEURAL ENGINE is an independent research project dedicated to mapping the landscape of neuromorphic computing and computational neuroscience.

Its purpose is simple: to make foundational papers, influential ideas, biological mechanisms, device technologies, and neuromorphic hardware systems easier to discover, navigate, and understand. The archive is assembled through manual curation from academic literature, including arXiv, IEEE, ACM, Springer, Nature, and other research publishers. Content is expanded continuously as new papers are reviewed and incorporated into the knowledge base.

Neural Engine is not a blog, news site, or paper dump. It is an attempt to build a structured map of a field that spans brain biology, neuron models, learning rules, network dynamics, memristive devices, and neuromorphic hardware.

This project is independently maintained and is not affiliated with any institution.
